One bird sacrificed its glorious past of being an apex prehistoric predator to being a domesticated meat bird

Ingrediants Required

  • water
  • protein
  • fats

Steps for Preparing

  1. Chicken companies buy female chicks, called pullets, at one day old.
  2. Pullets are raised to maturity in breeder houses, where they produce fertilized eggs.
  3. The eggs are incubated and hatched in hatcheries.
  4. The chicks are raised on farms, where they eat, drink, and roam freely.
  5. When they reach the desired weight, they are transported to processing plants.
